About CitizenConnect

We're revolutionizing citizen request supply chains through blockchian technology, ensuring transparency, trust, and traceability from citizen to the responsible authority.

Our Story

CitizenConnect was born from a simple observation: the social services supply chain is one of the most opaque and complex systems in global commerce and governance. Despite being the backbone of modern technology, from smartphones to electric vehicles, the journey of social services from provider to the citizen remained largely invisible.

Our founders, having witnessed firsthand the challenges of supply chain transparency in both blockchain and social service industries, envisisoned a solution that could bring the same level of transparency and trust to social service provision that blockchain brought into digital transactions.

Today, CitizenConnect stands as the first comprehensive blockchain platform dedicated to request traceability, serving social industries, citizens and social agensts worldwide with unprecedented transparency and security.

Built for the future

Our platdorm is designed to scale with the growing demands of digital governance, ensuring that transparency and trust become the standard, not the exception.
